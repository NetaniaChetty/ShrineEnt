About Chune With Sone

Chune With Sone is the entertainment arm of SHRINE ENTERPRISES, led by the dynamic and charismatic Sonehri. With years of experience hosting events across South Africa, Sonehri has built a reputation for electrifying crowds and creating memories that last a lifetime.

Whether it's a wedding, corporate function, birthday party, or cultural celebration, Chune With Sone brings the perfect blend of professionalism, personality, and pure entertainment. We don't just MC events — we create experiences.

What We Offer

  • Professional MC Services – Sonehri brings unmatched energy and professionalism to every event, keeping your guests engaged and entertained from start to finish.
  • Weddings & Engagements – Make your special day truly unforgettable with a seasoned MC who knows how to read the room and keep the celebration flowing.
  • Corporate Events – From product launches to year-end functions, Chune With Sone delivers polished and dynamic hosting for corporate gatherings.
  • Parties & Private Events – Cultural celebrations, birthdays, and private parties. We tailor our services to match your vision, budget and vibe.
  • Event Planning Support – Beyond MC duties, we help coordinate your event timeline to ensure everything runs smoothly on the big day.

Every event is unique. We tailor our services to match your vision, budget and vibe — no cookie-cutter packages here.
